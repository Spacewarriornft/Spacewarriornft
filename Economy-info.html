Economy Phase 1

Deflationary (Pre-Game)
The economy of phase 1 will be relatively simple. We will use a relatively basic token. With a total of 5% fee (Burn, Reward, Marketing)*. This will allow us to start the basic economy while we build the game economy contracts and make sure with Paladin that there are no errors and that everything is safe for our community. We will implement each function one at a time afterward. Every contract and every aspect of the economy will have to be safe and with no malfunctions whatsoever. The security of our community's investment is our top priority. Our entire system is based on the smooth running of the game. The team will only generate profit on the transaction volume (1%). The team or even the founder will not hold any tokens before the launch of phase 1 of the economy. They will have to buy when the liquidity is created at the same the community will be able to buy. The only tokens that will be in circulation that will not be in the liquidity is the 20% of tokens distributed to the minter. This is to limit the possibility of dumping by the presalers or the team.

Moving from phase 1 to phase 2

Moving from phase 1 to phase 2 will have no impact on the price of the token. We will airdrop SpaceCoin(2) To all SpaceCoin(1) Holder at a ratio of 1 to 1. Then we are going to take the liquidity from SpaceCoin(1) and break the lp. Then the same amount of SpaceCoin(2) will be minted and pared with the Avax from the SpaceCoin(1) liquidity. Doing it like this will make the Spacecoin(2) MarketCap the same as SpaceCoin(1) MarketCap and SpaceCoin(2) will also have the same price as SpaceCoin(1) last Traded price before we remove de liquidity to make the liquidy for SpaceCoin(2)

1- Remove liquidity from SpaceCoin(1)

3- Take liquidity from SpaceCoin(1) And Make Liquidity For SpaceCoin(2)
