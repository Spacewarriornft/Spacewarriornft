Game Info

We will share more information about Phase 2 at the appropriate time. We want to try as much as possible to keep certain aspects of the economy secret until the launch. It will hopefully limit forks or other projects that want to copy this one.

3 races- Human - Alien-​​Secret race.

At the game launch, there will be 2 races available. Humans and Aliens. Only the 10k Human and 10k Alien will be available to start. All other Nft will be Farme by the community. With farming, a 3rd race will appear.

Aesthetic equipment:

Several aesthetic types of equipment will be released. They will be simply aesthetic items. They will not give any advantage to other players in the game. The sale of these items will be done in the game and can be resold on the secondary market. They will be mostly world event items. You can then customize your NFT to your liking. Every aesthetic item will have a predetermined limit of MINT. Some items will be even rarer than others. No event series will be repeated once the event is over. The distribution of funds from the sale of cosmetics will be as follows. 50% will be Burn - 25% will go to charity - 20% to the artist - 5% development.

Daily task and PVM:

You will be able to have your team fight against different monsters. You will be rewarded with a token for each victory. You will also have a chance to get rare battle equipment or special farming equipment that will give you discounts on the Nft farming fee or even the possibility that your human and alien will both survive the mating. The special farming items will be extremely rare. A really limited amount will be available at the beginning and it will have an extremely rare drop rate afterward. We don't want this to have too much impact on the overall economy of the game. But will be a very good asset for the more daring among you!

Combat equipment/farming:

All combat equipment will deteriorate over time and the number of fights. You will have to repair them using tokens. Each weapon will give you different combat skills. They will also have different statistics in the game. They will all have advantages over some monsters as well as disadvantages depending on the class of weapon used. It will be possible to get different and additional combat equipment during the daily task and Pvm. It will also be possible to get farming equipment. But these will be extremely rare. Since they will give a significant advantage to the holder. Other weapons like arrows and bows will be incorporated at the launch of the game.

Farming NFT:

It will be possible to mate a male and a female of the same breed. You will then get a baby or an egg depending on the breed used. After some time the baby or egg will become an adult. It would then have the basic equipment (Armor, Knives, Boots). Any other aesthetic or combat equipment will have to be bought or obtained during Daily tasks. It will also be possible to sell or buy combat and costume equipment on the secondary market. It will also be possible to mate the two races together. This will create a race superior to the first two. But there will be a price to pay to create this superior race. More information is in the Burn Nft section.

PVP: Alien vs Human vs 3race:

You will have the chance to have your team fight against other people from the community. You will be able to bet on the outcome of the fight. 90% of the bet tokens will be distributed to the winner and 10% will be burned forever. More information is to come later this year. This feature will not be available at the release of the game.

Burn Token:

The token Burn for phase 2 will be simple to incorporate. We will take what most big games use to make a profit. But instead of taking a direct profit. Instead, we will use this method to burn tokens. First, there would be an Nft Farming fee. This fee will then be burned directly. No % will be kept by the team. The battle equipment will have a chance to break. You will then have to use tokens to repair them. These will then be directly burned. There will be cosmetic equipment that will be sold during the year for different events. The cosmetic equipment will only be sold in-game and will have a limited quantity. It can be sold later on the secondary market. The distribution of the profit from the sale of cosmetics will be as follows. 50% will be Burn - 25% will go to charity - 20% to the artist - 5% development. There will also be 10% of the tokens bet during Pvp burned each time. 90% will go to the winner.

Burn Nft:

We need to incorporate a burn to ensure that the market is not overloaded with characters. This is where the 3rd race will come into play. You will be able to mate 1 female and 1 male from each race together. This will create a superior race. The price paid. One of the 2 NFT will succumb to the mating and will be destroyed forever. You will then find yourself in possession of the superior race and you will still possess one of the original 2 Nft vales. Either the human or the alien. The rate according to which has more chances to survive will be determined by the difference of the 2 populations. If the difference of the Alien is much greater than that of the human. The alien will then be burned. This will not control the population of the 3rd race. The 3rd race will of course be more powerful than the other two. We must then find a solution to control the population of this 3rd race. First of all, it will be impossible to mate this 3rd race together. The only way to get one will be to mate a human and an alien together or to buy them on the secondary market. The burn of this 3rd race will be in the form of Buyback burn. Each month we will buy back a part of this 3rd race at the price floor of the secondary market. This will also have the effect of stimulating the secondary economy.
